# Characters (internal reference)

Internal design tool only — not surfaced to the player anywhere. Summaries of the five love
interests and the main character, for guiding writing and design choices. Not currently reflected
in any game system (no VN/relationship content yet — see `docs/design/systems.md`).

## The Alchemist (player character — tea green)

The player chooses her name and House (and thus her color), but her situation stays constant: she's
in the middle of strange fate. Her fey friend Garnet has led her perhaps somewhat astray, as the fey
are wont to do, into running an alchemy shop called Vial Story — abandoned by its former owner, and
the owner before that, and the owner before that. The place is definitely cursed. A little bedraggled
and hapless, she's already over her head running Vial Story at all, let alone with wild fey magic
involved. With the way strange coincidences converge on Vial Story, and the five bachelorettes
showing up at her door all at once... it might be more than she can handle. But she and Garnet will
do their best.

## Callie — Calissa "Callie" Nightbloom (Dolphin House, teal)

Third-year student of Restoration. Third daughter of a family of powerful arcanists; her grandmother
pioneered several key bioarcane engineering techniques. Upbeat party girl, always ready to go out and
have fun — a legendary socialite with connections from the most esoteric Finders to the
highest-ranking administrators. Under the bubbly exterior, she's deeply sentimental, feels like she's
failed an important family legacy, worries she's never really enough, and keeps people at a surface
level because she's convinced they'll get tired of her if they see her serious side.

## Larissa Pereira (Boar House, forest)

Second-year student of Wandcrafting. First-generation arcanist who has worked very hard to get where
she is — a well-respected wandcrafter whose designs have helped her House in countless games, featured
in wand studios all through the Citadel and highly sought-after by every metric of success. Deeply
insecure underneath: not sure how she's been successful, worried it's just luck that will run out and
she'll spend all her time chasing her fifteen minutes of fame again. Her parents, Brazilian immigrants
chasing the typical American dream, don't "get" the arcane and constantly demean her arcanist goals.
Sweet, compassionate girl-next-door type who pours herself into making things better for everyone
around her — impossible not to like.

## Hae-rin Kang (Dragon House, plum)

Fourth-year student of Artificy. Fiery, intense leader type. Her parents are first-generation
arcanists who moved to the US for higher arcane opportunities in the greater Starfall area; as eldest
daughter, she was expected to lead and do great things, and by all measures she has — solid artifact
designs, a mentorship lined up directly with Julianna Valey after graduation, an expert dragon racer,
proven in the duel circuit, every teacher's favorite. Despite it all, she constantly feels like she's
failing: nothing is ever enough, every microscopic mistake feels life-ending. All her self-worth is
defined by achievement, so every slip becomes an ultimatum on her as a person.

## Daniela Shadowlance (Eagle House, crimson)

Third-year student of Chronomancy. From the powerful but often merciless Shadowlance magic family —
cool, aloof, detached, very powerful with her magic. An exacting honors student with top marks in
every class who's contributed to higher-level magic research with her professors. Underneath the
polished façade she's the guitarist and lead vocalist of a spectral rock act under her stage name
Eclipse, letting out aggression that doesn't fit the "perfect student" image. Underneath the cover of
her relentless competence, she's slowly dying to a timestream confluence she can't escape.

## Lyra Burnwicke (Scorpion House, gold)

Second-year Diviner with an incredible gift for divination — but she doesn't want to use it. She wants
to do Enchantment instead, but felt it would be a waste to let go of her divination gift, especially
under the pressure of being related to the famous diviner Calla Burnwicke. Soft, sensitive, deeply
artistic — prefers painting, playing music, and crafting beautiful magic items. Haunted by visions of
terrible things happening, which she uses to help stop them; recognized enough for how much she helps
people that professors don't even look up when she suddenly sprints out of class after a vision. But
the visions are so awful and haunting, and the responsibility so heavy, that quietly, secretly, she
wishes she didn't have this power.
